Serbian Migration

1000.00 USD

Serbian Migration, or the full title Serbian Migration under Patriarch Arsenije III Čarnojević, is a series of four similar oil paintings by Serbian realist painter Paja Jovanović, depicting Serbs led by Serbian Patriarch Arsenije III Čarnojević leaving Old Serbia during the Great Serbian Migrations of 1690-1691.
